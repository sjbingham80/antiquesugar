Do you have cool old clothes to sell?

Recycling your vintage clothing at Antique Sugar is a great way to help out the environment while earning a little extra cash or trade towards a fab new-to-you vintage collection. Simply give us a call to set up a time to meet with our in store buyer.

Call us for an appointment!

Our buyer is typically in the shop Wednesday-Friday and accepts appointments between noon and 6pm. Give us a call at (602)277-5765 and we'll set up a time for you to come in and show us what you've got.

Live across town? Across the state? Not sure if what you have is vintage? We still wanna see the goods. If you snap some quick phone pics and text them to us we'll be able to tell you if we're likely to buy them before you make the trek.

We're always looking for:

We generally buy clothing from the 1990's and earlier. The older the better.

  • dresses
  • blouses and casual tops
  • skirts, shorts, fancy pants
  • bell bottoms
  • mens suits, jackets, dress/casual shirts
  • loud polyester
  • plaid pants
  • vintage denim
  • cool old T-shirts
  • costume jewelry
  • anything with rhinestones, sequins or fringe

    • We love the outrageous as much as the classy


This is just a sampling of the types of items we take, so if you have clothes or accessories that aren’t on this list, just ask!
